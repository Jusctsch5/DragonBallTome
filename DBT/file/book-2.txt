Pilot's Club members are allowed unlimited access to

#####GFees                                                                     
Membership dues amount to 1000zn per day. To maintain                          
membership in good standing, Pilot's Club members are

at all times. Failure to do so may result in forfeiture                        
of hanger, and all materials stored therein.
